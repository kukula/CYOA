Annual Business Conference “ABC: Ukraine & Partners”

The first International Annual Business Conference “ABC: Ukraine & Partners” took place on June 13 – 14, 2013 in Kyiv with the participation of the Prime Minister of Ukraine Mykola Azarov and other members of the Ukrainian Government.  A wide range of governmental representatives, business and academia, financial, banking and investment institutions, diplomatic missions and media were invited to the conference.
