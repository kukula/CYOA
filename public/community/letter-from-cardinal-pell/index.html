Letter from Cardinal Pell

His Eminence Cardinal George Pell, Archbishop of Sydney, has sent a message of greeting to the Ukrainian Catholic Parish of St Andrew in Sydney on the occasion their feast day (Praznyk).

Perhaps of more significance, the cardinal offers his prayers and support for our Brothers and Sisters in the Freedom Maidan in Kyiv.
