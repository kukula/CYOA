Join the Invictus Flag Presentation in Sydney!

The Invictus Games is coming to Sydney in October this year. As part of festivities leading up to October, an Invictus Games Flag will be presented to the Ukrainian Community by Luke Foley MP. You can be apart of the Flag Presentation, it is happening this Saturday, August 25th in Sydney, 1.30pm at the Independence Festival. More details about the festival can be found here.

Following the presentation, there will be a community photo with Luke Foley MP, at 1.40pm. This photo will be featured on the Invictus Foundation website apart of the Fly the Flag campaign. An additional photo will be taken which will be published in an upcoming coffee table publication “Ukrainian Australians” honouring the 70th Anniversary of Ukrainian Settlement in Australia.

You’re welcome to wear Vyshyvanka!
