Holodomor Exhibition in Melbourne

[caption id="attachment_12092” align="alignnone” width="1024”] Holodomor Exhibition Melbourne[/caption]

This exhibition honours the 85th Anniversary of the Holodomor and features 26 Australian-Ukrainian survivors. Something not to be missed, we must not let the world forget this atrocity!

Exhibition is running from 8th-17th November with the gallery open Monday - Friday 11am to 5pm and Saturdays 11am-4pm (Not open on Sundays). Special opening night will happen 8th November, places limited, register here: holodomor85.eventbrite.com
