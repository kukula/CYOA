Inter-Airlines and TravelWiz Newsletter

Inter-Airlines and TravelWiz have just published their latest newsletter.  Amongst other services, these two companies organize travel packages to Ukraine.

The Managing Director, Adel Bassili,  is a good friend of the Ukrainian community in Australia, and recently has offered to donate, along with Thai Airlines, a return flight ticket (Australia - Bangkok) to assist in sponsorship of the ‘Hopak in the Park’ event  in April.

The newletter can be read here:  inter-airlines_newsletter0311

Please note that the TravelWiz Flight Booking Engine is now available at   travelwiz.com.au
