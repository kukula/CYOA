Media coverage of rally in Melbourne over Ukraine crisis

The Ukrainian community of Melbourne along with several other communities offering their support staged a rally in the CBD on 4th March 2014 protesting against the illegal invasion of Ukraine by Russian forces.  Over 500 demonstrators took part in a march and assembly.  The event was covered by TV news channels, radio stations, print and internet media.  Links to a small sample of these are provided here:
