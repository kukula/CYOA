Holodomor speech in Parliament of Australia

On the 21st November 2013 the Hon. Philip Ruddock MP, the Government Chief Whip in the House of Representatives, the longest serving Member of the Parliament of Australia and a steadfast friend of the Ukrainian community, spoke in the Parliament about the Ukrainian Holodomor famine.
