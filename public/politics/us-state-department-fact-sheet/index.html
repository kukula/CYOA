US State Department Fact Sheet

The US Department of State has produced the first iteration of a new “Public Diplomacy Tool Kit on Ukraine” that it will be deploying to its Department messengers to assist them in promoting accurate narratives about the crisis in the Ukraine.  The document contains excellent information regarding the true facts about events in Ukraine, as well as containing links to other valuable supporting sites.

A PDF copy of the Tool Kit can be viewed here:  US Toolkit on Ukraine Narratives 2014-03-14
