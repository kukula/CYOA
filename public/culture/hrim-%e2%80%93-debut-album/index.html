HRIM – Debut Album

Hrim, the Ukrainian band from New York City that performed at the Hopak in the Park festival in April 2011 is happy to announce the online release of their debut album.  To purchase the album, please visit www.CDBaby.com/hrim or search “Hrim” on the iTunes store.

For more information about Hrim, go to http://www.reverbnation.com/hrim or search them on Facebook, Twitter, and MySpace.

The band is excited to be coming back to Melbourne in April of 2012!
