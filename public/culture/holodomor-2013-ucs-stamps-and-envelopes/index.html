Holodomor 2013 - UCS stamps and envelopes

The Ukrainian Collectibles Society, in recognition of the 80th Anniversary of the Holodomor (the artificial famine and genocide in Ukraine of 1932-1933), is releasing commercial and collectible souvenirs as a tangible and collectible philatelic record.

Information Sheet with details for special commemorative stamp and souvenir envelopes:  Information Sheet No.7 (Holodomor)

Order Form for this issue:  Holodomor order form

The very last day to order and pay is Monday night, 11 November 2013.
