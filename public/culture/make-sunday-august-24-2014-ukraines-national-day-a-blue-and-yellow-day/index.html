MAKE SUNDAY AUGUST 24 2014 UKRAINE'S NATIONAL DAY - A BLUE AND YELLOW DAY

The Australian Federation of Ukrainian Organisations calls on the Australia's Ukrainian and broader community to support President Poroshenko's call to make Sunday BLUE AND YELLOW DAY IN SUPPORT OF UKRAINE Fly a flag, use the colours creatively in fashion, in decorations, in decorating houses , cars , offices State Organisations and individuals should think creatively how they could promote the colours in public places – Time is short but ideas normally flow quickly – Let us know your ideas Post on Facebook and email your suggestions
