XIV National Ukrainian Festival

The XIV National Ukrainian Festival will be held in Sydney on 7th – 9th June 2013.

The Festival will be held under the auspice of the Australian Federation of Ukrainian Organisations and hosted by the Ukrainian Council of NSW.

This year's theme: 65th Anniversary of Ukrainian Settlement in Australia. Past, Present, Future!
