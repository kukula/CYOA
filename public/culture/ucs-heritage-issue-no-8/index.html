UCS Heritage issue no. 8

The Ukrainian Collectibles Society (UCS) of Australia has issued a Press Release announcing the eighth stamp and envelope in the Ukrainian Heritage in Australia Personalised Stamp™ and souvenir first day of issue envelope series on 1 June 2013.  The new stamp is dedicated to the Ukrainian Autocephalic Orthodox Church (UAOC) in Diaspora, Diocese of Australia and New Zealand.
