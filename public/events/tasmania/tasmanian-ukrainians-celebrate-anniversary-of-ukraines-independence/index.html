Tasmanian Ukrainians Celebrate Anniversary of Ukraine's Independence

On Sunday 28th of August 2011 the Association of Ukrainians in Tasmania, one of the smaller Ukrainian communities in Australia, whole-heartedly celebrated the 20th Anniversary of Ukraine's Independence. 

The commemorative event included a church service, keynote speeches and cultural performances. 

The day was enjoyed by all present.
