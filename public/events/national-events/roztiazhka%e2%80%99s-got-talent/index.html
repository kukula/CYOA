Roztiazhka’s Got Talent

Roztiazhka, a Ukrainian dance company from Perth in Western Australia, has recently appeared on Channel 7’s talent show Australia’s Got Talent.  The judges were highly impressed, and Roztiazhka have got through to the next round.  Congratulations to all the dancers on a fantastic performance.  Best wishes.  Щасливо!

Watch the performance here: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CgSJVU_tj18

Roztiazka also took part in the recent highly-successful Hopak in the Park dance spectacular.
