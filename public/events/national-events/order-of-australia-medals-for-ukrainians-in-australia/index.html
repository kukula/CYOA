Order of Australia Medals for Ukrainians in Australia

Congratulating Ukrainians in Australia who Received Order of Australia Medals as part of the Queen's Birthday Honours List.

The Australian Federation of Ukrainian Organisations congratulates Ukrainians who received

Order of Australia Medals

Rev Fr Zenon Chorkawyj OAM for services to the Ukrainian Catholic Church

Stephen Chomyn OAM for services to the Ukrainian Community in Brisbane

Anna Stefaniuk OAM for services to education through multicultural initiatives to the Ukrainian community

The AFUO congratulates you for your achievement and for doing the community proud.
