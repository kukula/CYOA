Prayer Vigil in Melbourne

A prayer vigil in support of the Ukrainian people’s movement in Ukraine will be held on: SATURDAY 7TH DECEMBER 1.00PM UKRAINIAN CATHOLIC CHURCH - STS PETER AND PAUL CATHEDRAL 35 CANNING STREET NORTH MELBOURNE

Whether you are of Ukrainian or other background, join in prayer as concerned Australians.

Gather to show solidarity with Ukraine and to pray for peaceful and positive change.
