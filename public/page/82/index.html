Book Launch in Perth

Wed, Aug 15, 2012 Book Launch in Perth: Silent Memories, Traumatic Lives Ukrainian Migrant Refugees in Western Australia****__ by Dr Lesa Melnyczuk ** **—————————————————————————————————————-  This is the first work to tell the story of the Ukrainian survivors who were accepted as migrant refugees to Western Australia after World War II. The experience of these people was one of enormous struggle where only the strongest survived, to now finally share their story. By bringing their stories together for the first time we begin to understand the refugee experience of post-war migrant Ukrainians who settled in Western Australia.
