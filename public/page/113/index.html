Cultural Diversity Week

Mon, Mar 21, 2011 The Multicultural Commission of the Victorian State Government is promoting a Cultural Diversity Week. This will be held from 19-27 March 2011. During the Week, Victorians come together to share aspects of their culture, faith and language and celebrate the benefits that multiculturalism brings to the State of Victoria. For more information see: http://www.multicultural.vic.gov.au/projects-and-initiatives/cultural-diversity-week
