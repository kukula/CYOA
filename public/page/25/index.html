Theo Andruszko's Contribution to our Community

Wed, Mar 23, 2016 Theo Andruszko who passed away in November 2015 was awarded “Senior Volunteer of the Year” posthumously. Alexander Andruszko accepted the award on behalf of his late father. Four members of the Andruszko family (one of whom flew in from Sydney) were there to represent their father. For five decades, Theo was an integral part of Adelaide’s Ukrainian community. He documented the community’s history in several books, edited the Ukrainian Association monthly newsletter for eight years, volunteered his time and expertise at a state and federal level, and acted as a mentor.
