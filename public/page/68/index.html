Ukrainian Independence Day Concert Melbourne 2013

Wed, Aug 21, 2013 The Ukrainian community in Melbourne together with many of its friends celebrated the 22nd anniversary of Ukrainian Independence Day on Sunday 18th August 2013. The venue, as in previous years, was at the BMW Edge in Federation Square. Visitors started arriving before lunch-time, eager to examine the various displays and grab a bowl of borshch or varenyky before the official start. This year the event coordinator was Peter Mazurcuk, with Taras Galas performing the duties of Master of Ceremonies.
