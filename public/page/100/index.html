National Youth Week Grants Program

Mon, Sep 19, 2011 National Youth Week (NYW) is the largest celebration of young people on the Australian youth calendar. The event provides an opportunity for young people to express their ideas, showcase their talents, have their voices heard on issues that affect their lives, celebrate their positive contribution to the community and most importantly, have fun. The celebrations will run from 13 - 22 April 2012.  Funding will be available for organisations to host a NYW event that brings young people together.

People of Australia Ambassador

Thu, Sep 8, 2011 Nominations now open to be a People of Australia Ambassador  Culturally and linguistically diverse individuals and communities are invited to nominate a person or themselves to be considered as a People of Australia Ambassador.  As part of Australia’s new multicultural policy, up to 40 people will be selected as ambassadors for a 12-month period to provide ‘grass roots’ advice to the Australian Multicultural Council (AMC) about effective initiatives that promote inclusion and leverage diversity in our communities.

UCS PRESS RELEASE September 2011

Wed, Sep 7, 2011 The Ukrainian Collectibles Society (UCS) of Australia has issued a Press Release in respect of the staging of the 3rd National Ukrainian Collectibles Exhibition UCSPEX 2011 at the Ukrainian Youth Hall in Lidcombe, Sydney, between 27‐28th August 2011. This event was held in conjunction with celebrations conducted by the Ukrainian Council of NSW to observe the 20th Anniversary of the Renewal of the Independence of Ukraine.
