AFUO Media Release – Ukraine Crisis

Sun, Mar 2, 2014 [ ](http://www.ozeukes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/zCYOA-LOGO-7-star-color-150pxls.jpg)'PUTIN, GET OUT OF UKRAINE.
