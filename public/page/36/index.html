Wed, Apr 1, 2015 **AFUO met the Deputy Head of the foreign affairs committee ** The AFUO delegation and World Congress of Ukrainian representatives met Ms Hanna Hopko, People’s Deputy and Chair of the Ukrainian parliament’s (Rada) Foreign Affairs Committee.
