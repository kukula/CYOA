Ukrainian Catholic Church now a recognised denomination in Australia

Mon, Jul 1, 2013 [ ](http://www.ozeukes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/EparchyCrest_thumb.jpg) Governor-General proclaims Ukrainian Catholic Church in Australia a recognised denomination. Since its inception over 50 years ago, the Ukrainian Greek-Catholic Eparchy in Australia has been regarded by the Commonwealth government as a part of the Roman Catholic Church for the purposes of registration as a religious entity. Such registration brings with it certain authority and responsibilities, and particularly the power to nominate ministers of religion as authorised marriage celebrants.
